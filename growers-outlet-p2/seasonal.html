Seasonal Offerings

Our family owned business has been established for over 40 years. We specialize in bringing a year round farmers market to the community- which includes our seasonal features that make our store unique.

Garden Center

Open February to October

A huge selection of flowers to add color and excitement to your garden. From hanging baskets to gardening tools to vegetable plants (including heirlooms and organics), we definitely have what your garden needs.

New inventory is delivered several times a week, so visit often to see what’s new.

The highest quality, but priced reasonably.

Pumpkin Patch

Open through October

LWe provide a free to explore corn maze and pumpkin patch, great for photos and a fun day out with the family.

Looking for decorations? We have corn stalks, gourds, Indian corn, hay bales, and potted mums.

Or if you just have a sweet tooth on a chilly day, come on in for some apple cider and caramel apple supplies!

Christmas Tree Lot

Open November through December

Looking for the perfect tree during the holiday season? Look no further than our tree lot! We have hundreds of fresh cut Christmas trees and decorative wreathes straight from the tree farm.

Take your pick from Douglas Firs, Noble Firs, and Grand Firs. Trees are priced by size and type, so there’s definitely one that will fit any kind of budget.

Stop on in to take your tree home, easy peasy!
