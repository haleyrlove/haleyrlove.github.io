Club Penguin

Homework for February 1st

I decided to reminisce about Club Penguin because I used to play it allll the time in elementary school. It was to the point that I would ask for giftcards so that I could trick out my penguin with cool clothes and have a cool igloo house full of pets. Looking back, I think the early 2005-2007 website was really iconic in it's design. It always had this signature blue background and buttons with white all capital text that resembled comic sans. Otherwise all the text looked like it was in some generic Arial font. It was iconic because of how nostalgic it was, and it's interesting to know that even if the website itself wasn't the most well put together- all kids cared about was the game content.

For my project one website, I was interested in creating an informational site about gachapon machines. Gachapon (ガチャポン), also known as gashapon (ガシャポン), are toy dispensers in Japan that resemble Western toy vending machines. You insert a coin, usually between 100 to 500 yen, and turn the handle to release a capsule.


I'm interested in this topic because I'm going to Japan this year, and I've always been excited over collectibles and toy machines. I think it'll be something fun that people don't really think about. There's no particular website I'm modeling after, I just know that I want something colorful and engaging, because that's my style.
